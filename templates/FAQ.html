Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: How do I register on the platform?

A1: Users can sign up by providing their email address, phone number (USA only), and password.

Q2: What is the email verification process?

A2: Upon registration, users receive a confirmation email with a verification link to their provided phone number.

Q3: How do I verify my driver's license?

A3: After email verification, users are prompted to upload a photo or scan of their driver's license through a secure portal.

Q4: How does phone number verification work?

A4: Users receive a verification code via SMS to the phone number provided during registration. They need to enter the code on the platform to confirm their phone number.

Q5: When do I receive $15 reward?

A5: 24 Hrs after you complete the verification process, you will be prompted to enter your PayPal account information to receive a $15 reward for your participation.

Q6: How does the referral program work?

A6: Users are provided with a unique invite code that they can share with friends and family. When a new user signs up using the invite code, both the new user and the original user receive a benefit.

Q7: How can I accumulate rewards?

A7: Users can accumulate rewards in their accounts based on survey participation and successful referrals.

Q8: How is my PayPal account verified for reward distribution?

A9: Users will receive a verification link or code to their registered email address. They need to verify their PayPal account to ensure the accuracy of the provided information.
